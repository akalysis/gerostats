Digital garden

Data stories, methods, tools and cultivated notes.

This is where I collect the pieces that need a bit of room: data stories, public-facing methods, teaching tools and knowledge-base guides about ageing, care, place and inequality. Some entries are finished essays; others are planted here so they can be revised, connected and grown over time.

Gerostats keeps the garden language deliberately: statistics are not only final outputs. They are working notes, technical decisions, local memories, diagrams, equations, maps and tools that become more useful as they are connected.
