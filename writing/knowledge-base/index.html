The Knowledge Base.

A structured, living repository of statistical methods, software paradigms and epidemiological workflows.

Unlike the broader dispatches in the garden, this section is designed to act as an instructional reference for students, early-career researchers and analysts dealing with high-dimensional health datasets.

The guides here do not rely on clean, generic car-mileage datasets. They focus on the mess and mathematical reality of epidemiology, ageing, survival analysis and health services research.

Where to start?

If you are starting medical statistics or epidemiology for the first time, Stata remains a powerful environment for reproducible and precise inference.
